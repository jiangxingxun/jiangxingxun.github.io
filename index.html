About Me (CV)(resume)

Since September 2018, I have become a master student of Affective Information Processing Lab(AIPL) at School of Biological Sciences and Medical Engineering and also affiliated with Key Laboratory of Child Development and Learning Science of Ministry of Education, Southeast University, under the supervision of Prof. Wenming Zheng.

Previously, I received my B.S. degree in Electrical Engineering (Smart Grid Information Engineering) from Nanjing University of Posts and Telecommunications in June 2017, under the supervision of Prof. Yingjun Wu.
